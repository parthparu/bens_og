Forecasting Model Tournament (RMSE)

Comparing out-of-sample Root Mean Squared Error across candidate architectures. Lower values represent more accurate projections.

💡 Pipeline Modeling Notes
  • Random Forest leads the leaderboard (RMSE: ~554K), closely followed by Gradient Boosting (Huber Loss).
  • Stacking Ensembles underperform single tree models slightly, suggesting potential overfitting due to the modest sample size (122 weeks).
  • ARIMAX shows extremely high variance, indicating that pure statistical time series struggles with high-dimensionality spend features without strict regularizations.
